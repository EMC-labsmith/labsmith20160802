The steps about how to do pxe is as follows:

Step 1: Copy the image to 10.62.34.100:/images.

Step 2: Reserve device (Oberon or Beachcomber), and then click the "Go PXE" button.

Step 3: On the PXE page (above), firstly confirm the infomation and then select image by using drop-down lists "OS file path".

Step 4: Last, confirm the infomation again, and click the blue button below.

Step 5: OS file will be deleted in 90 days.
